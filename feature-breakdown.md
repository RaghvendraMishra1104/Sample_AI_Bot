# Feature Breakdown — AI Study Assistant

---

## Feature 1: Note Summarizer

**Description:**
Students upload their notes (PDF or plain text) and receive a concise,
bullet-point summary generated by AI.

**User Benefit:** Saves hours of manual note-making; great for last-minute revision.

### Sub-features:
- Text paste input box
- PDF upload (up to 5MB)
- Adjustable summary length (Short / Medium / Detailed)
- Copy summary button
- Download as PDF option

### Acceptance Criteria:
- Summary generated in under 10 seconds
- Maintains key points without changing meaning
- Works for inputs up to 2000 words

---

## Feature 2: Q&A Assistant

**Description:**
A conversational AI interface where students type any academic question
and receive an accurate, easy-to-understand answer instantly.

**User Benefit:** Replaces the need for a tutor for concept-based doubts.

### Sub-features:
- Text input with 500-character limit
- Follow-up question support (multi-turn conversation)
- Subject tagging on each answer
- Bookmark answer option
- "Report wrong answer" button

### Acceptance Criteria:
- Response in under 5 seconds
- Answer is contextually relevant to the question
- Follow-up questions retain previous context

---

## Feature 3: Assignment Generator

**Description:**
Students or teachers select a subject and topic, choose difficulty level,
and the AI generates a set of practice questions automatically.

**User Benefit:** Provides unlimited practice material without manual effort.

### Sub-features:
- Subject selector (Maths, Science, English, History, etc.)
- Topic text input
- Difficulty level: Easy / Medium / Hard
- Number of questions selector (5 / 10 / 20)
- Show/Hide answers toggle
- Download as PDF

### Acceptance Criteria:
- Questions are unique and relevant to the entered topic
- Difficulty levels produce noticeably different question styles
- Works for major school and college subjects

---

## Feature 4: Concept Explainer

**Description:**
Users enter any concept or topic and receive a clear explanation tailored
to their selected level — simple (beginner) or detailed (advanced).

**User Benefit:** Makes complex concepts accessible; great for visual/simple learners.

### Sub-features:
- Concept name input
- Explanation level toggle: Simple / Detailed
- Real-world analogy included in response
- Related concepts suggestions
- Text-to-speech (read aloud) — v2

### Acceptance Criteria:
- Simple mode uses layman language with analogies
- Detailed mode includes technical depth
- Always includes at least one example

---

## Feature 5: Chat History

**Description:**
All user interactions with the AI are saved and organized so students
can revisit, search, and manage their study sessions.

**User Benefit:** Acts as a personal study log; reduces repeated questions.

### Sub-features:
- Chronological list of all sessions
- Search bar (keyword/subject filter)
- Bookmark / star important answers
- Delete individual chats or clear all
- Group by Subject or Date

### Acceptance Criteria:
- History loads in under 2 seconds
- Search returns relevant results accurately
- Bookmarked items are easily accessible
