# Product Brief — AI Study Assistant

## Product Name
AI Study Assistant (EduBot)

## Overview
AI Study Assistant is a mobile and web application designed to help students
study smarter using artificial intelligence. It acts as a personal academic
companion that summarizes notes, answers questions, generates practice
assignments, and explains concepts in simple language — available 24/7.

## Problem Statement
Students often struggle with:
- Understanding complex topics without immediate teacher support
- Spending too much time making notes from long textbooks
- Lack of personalized practice questions
- No affordable, always-available academic help

## Solution
An AI-powered app that acts like a smart study buddy — simplifying learning,
saving time, and making studying more interactive and effective.

## Target Audience
- School students (Grade 6–12)
- College and university students
- Competitive exam aspirants (JEE, NEET, UPSC, etc.)
- Self-learners and working professionals upskilling

## Core Features
1. **Note Summarizer** — Upload notes/PDFs and get crisp summaries
2. **Q&A Assistant** — Ask any academic question and get instant answers
3. **Assignment Generator** — Auto-generate practice questions by topic
4. **Concept Explainer** — Get simplified explanations with examples
5. **Chat History** — Review past queries and answers
6. **Subject Selection** -Personalized help per subject

## Platforms
- Mobile App (Android & iOS)
- Web App (Browser-based)

## Business Model
- Freemium (basic features free, premium for unlimited use)
- Student subscription plans (monthly/yearly)

## Success Metrics
- Daily Active Users (DAU)
- Number of queries answered per day
- User retention rate after 30 days
- Average session time
- Premium conversion rate
